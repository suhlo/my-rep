Welcome to Text Classification

Text classification is the process of assigning labels or categories to text based on its content. It is widely used in natural language processing (NLP) tasks, such as spam detection, sentiment analysis, and language detection. Our application demonstrates text classification with a focus on language detection.

Classify Your Text

Why Text Classification?

Text classification helps automate the process of categorizing text data, which is essential for organizing and analyzing large volumes of text efficiently. Whether for filtering spam emails, analyzing customer feedback, or detecting the language of text, text classification is a key component of many data-driven applications.
